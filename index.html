What We Do

We offer employers expansive recruitment tools, students skill validation and upskilling resources, and universities advanced data collection and modern educational integrations.

STUDENTS

We offer Certification to validate and recognize the skills acquired by young professionals, a BAG Resume that showcases their experience-based learning and achievements, and Upskilling Resources to bridge the gap between academic learning and the practical skills demanded by the industry.

EMPLOYERS

We offer employers Unlimited Experiences to find the best talent, Unlimited Resumes to ensure a wide selection of candidates, and a dedicated Account Manager to streamline and optimize the hiring process.

UNIVERSITIES

We offer universities Unlimited Data Collection for comprehensive insights, LMS Integration for a seamless educational experience, and Teacher Upskilling to enhance the capabilities of the academic staff.

About Us

Mission

  • BAG Innovation aims to provide experience-based learning through gamification to help young people acquire market-relevant skills.
  • They strive to bridge the gap between outdated university curriculums and the skills demanded by employers.
  • The mission also includes reducing hiring costs for businesses by ensuring that young job seekers are well-prepared and equipped with the necessary skills.

Vision

  • BAG Innovation envisions expanding its digital platform to other countries in East Africa, beyond its current presence in Rwanda.
  • They plan to onboard a significant portion (30%) of the student market in Rwanda, indicating their ambition to reach a wide audience and make a significant impact.
  • BAG Innovation aims to partner with industry experts, including the creator of Candy Crush, to gamify career development and empower young people throughout their professional journeys.
